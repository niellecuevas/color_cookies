How to Play

Welcome to the Cookie Color Matching Game!

Game Objective

Your task is to click on the cookie that matches the color displayed on the screen. Earn points by clicking correctly and avoid mistakes to keep your lives intact.

Game Controls

  • Click on the cookies to select them.
  • Watch the timer closely â€“ you have 10 seconds to make a correct match.

Scoring and Progression

  • You earn 1 point for each correct match.
  • Every 5 correct matches, the game becomes more challenging.

Lives

You start with 5 lives. Each incorrect choice costs you a life. If you lose all lives, the game is over.

Timer

The timer counts down 10 seconds for each round. Act quickly and make the correct match before time runs out!

Game Over

If you lose all your lives, the game will end. Your score will be displayed, and you can click 'Try Again' to restart.

Tips & Tricks

  • Pay close attention to the color of the cookies!
  • Try to stay calm and focus on the correct color to avoid mistakes.
