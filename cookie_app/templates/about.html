About the Game

Color Cookies is a Stroop effect game, themed around colorful cookies and engaging cognitive challenges.

The Stroop effect demonstrates the interference in reaction time when the brain tries to process conflicting information - like when the word "Red" is written in blue ink.

Test your mental flexibility and have fun with these delicious-looking cookie challenges!

About the Developer

This game was created with passion by a solo developer who:

  • Drew all the logos and sprites
  • Created all the cookie designs
  • Produced some of the sound effects
  • Developed the game mechanics

A labor of love combining art, psychology, and game design!
